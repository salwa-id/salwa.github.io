Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees Celsius. Grease two 20cm cake pans with vegetable oil and lightly flour.
  2. Add flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, cocoa and salt to a large bowl and whisk to combine.
  3. Add eggs, milk, vanilla extract and vegetable oil to the dry mixture and stir well.
  4. Carefully add the boiling water to the batter and whisk until smooth.
  5. Evenly distribute the batter between the two pans and place in the oven for 30-35 minutes or until a cake tester comes out clean when inserted in the center. Let cool.
  6. Combine chocolate chips, salt and cocoa in a medium bowl. Heat heavy cream and bring to a simmer before pouring over chocolate mixture.
  7. Let sit for 5 minutes before mixing until smooth.
  8. Once the cake is completely cool, spread the ganache over the cake and serve.
